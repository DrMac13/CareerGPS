Interview Preparation

STAR Method

Answer interview questions clearly

Use Situation, Task, Action, and Result to structure behavioural interview answers.

This is useful for questions about teamwork, leadership, conflict, and problem solving.

Practice Interview Profile
Technical Prep

Prepare for role-specific questions

Review the tools and concepts connected to your target career path, such as SQL, Python, Power BI, Django, business analysis, or cloud basics.

Focus on explaining your thinking, not only giving short definitions.

Confidence

Improve delivery and confidence

Practise speaking in complete sentences, using examples, and connecting your answers to the job description.

Strong answers are specific, structured, and relevant.

CV & LinkedIn

CV

Graduate CV structure

Your CV should include contact details, career summary, education, projects, work experience, skills, and achievements.

Keep it focused on the role you are applying for.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn profile checklist

Use a professional headline, clear summary, updated education, relevant projects, skills, and a clean profile photo.

Your LinkedIn should support the same career direction as your CV.

Portfolio

Show proof of ability

Add GitHub projects, dashboards, case studies, database projects, or business analysis documents to show what you can actually do.

A portfolio helps employers trust your skills.

Career Paths

Data Analyst

Data Analyst roadmap

Start with Excel, SQL, data cleaning, dashboards, Power BI, Python, statistics, and business storytelling.

Build projects that show insight, not just charts.

Software Engineer

Software Engineer roadmap

Build foundations in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, databases, APIs, Git, and deployment.

Focus on building complete applications, not isolated code snippets.

Business Analyst

Business Analyst roadmap

Learn requirements gathering, process modelling, stakeholder communication, documentation, SQL basics, and system analysis.

Strong business analysts connect business needs to technical solutions.

Application Success Tips

Tailoring

Tailor every application

Match your CV, cover letter, and project examples to the skills and responsibilities in the job description.

Tracking

Track your applications

Keep record of where you applied, the application date, company name, role, status, and follow-up actions.

View Applications
Follow-up

Follow up professionally

If you have not heard back after a reasonable period, send a polite follow-up message asking about the status of your application.
