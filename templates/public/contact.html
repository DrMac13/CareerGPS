Before contacting us

Profile issues

Check that your institution, degree, location, skills and interests are complete. These fields affect your recommendations.

Recommendation issues

If recommendations look inaccurate, update your skills and interests first. CareerGPS uses this data to calculate matches.

Application tracking

Saved and tracked opportunities are connected to your logged-in account, so make sure you are signed in with the correct email.
