About CareerGPS

CareerGPS is a student career navigation platform designed to help students discover relevant opportunities, understand their skill gaps, prepare for interviews and track their career progress.

Why CareerGPS exists

The problem

Many students struggle to connect their studies, skills and interests to real career opportunities. Job boards often show too many listings without explaining why a role fits.

The solution

CareerGPS uses profile data, skills, interests and live opportunities to recommend better matches and highlight what the student should improve next.

The goal

The goal is to help students move from uncertainty to a clear career direction with practical actions they can take.

What CareerGPS helps with

Opportunity discovery

Students can view recommended opportunities based on their profile, skills, location and career interests.

Skill gap analysis

CareerGPS identifies matched and missing skills so students know what to learn before applying.

Application tracking

Students can save opportunities and track roles they have applied for.

Interview preparation

The platform supports interview practice and analytics to help students improve their readiness.

CareerGPS is built around one idea.

Students should not only find opportunities. They should understand what makes them ready for those opportunities and what steps to take next.
