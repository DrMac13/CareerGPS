Career journeys are different for everyone. These stories highlight how students can use skills, preparation and persistence to move closer to their goals.

A final-year Information Systems student focused on SQL, Power BI and data analysis projects. By building a portfolio and improving interview skills, they secured a graduate data analyst opportunity.

After exploring multiple career paths, a student identified business analysis as the best fit and focused on developing requirements gathering, communication and process modelling skills.

Lessons Learned

Consistency Matters

Small improvements made consistently over time often produce better results than short bursts of activity.

Skills Create Opportunities

Employers are looking for evidence of practical ability. Projects and portfolios help demonstrate those abilities.

Preparation Builds Confidence

Interview practice and preparation make it easier to communicate your strengths effectively.
